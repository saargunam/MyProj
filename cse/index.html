About the college

Sri Ramakrishna Engineering College Sri Ramakrishna Engineering College (SREC) is one among the most reputed Educational Institutions in Coimbatore region. The college has carved a niche in the engineering education by delivering graduates who make a difference in the industry and the society. Since its inception in the year 1994, the institution has been continuously striving to make an indelible mark in the higher education scenario by creating technocrats sound in academics and Value system. SREC is one of the seventeen flagship institutions managed by SNR Sons Charitable Trust which include Polytechnic College, Schools, Dental College, Colleges of Pharmacy, Physiotherapy, Arts & Science, and Advanced Training Institute. The institution is located in a serene environment suitable for uninterrupted learning, away from the hustle and bustle of the city. Multistoried buildings with the state of the art educational aids are spread over the 42 acres of land. SREC contributes to the higher education scenario in India by offering 9 under graduate programmes and 9 post graduate programmes in Engineering and Technology in addition to MBA housing 5000 plus students, passionately mentored by 329 dedicated faculty members. Six undergraduate programmes are accredited and reaccredited. Also two post graduate programmes are accredited by National Board of Accreditation, New Delhi. Being an autonomous institution, the curricula and syllabi are constantly updated by Stalwarts from the reputed institutions like ISRO, TCS, HCL, Robert Bosch, Vikram Sarabai Space Research Centre, Infosys and CSIR as members of Board of Studies. The institution strongly believes in enhancing and encouraging the research culture among faculty and students. 58 faculty members are qualified with doctoral degree and 91 are pursuing Ph.D programmes in various disciplines. 107 research papers are published in various reputed and refereed international and National Journals by our faculty during the year 2013-2014. A total amount of Rs.1,00,22,000/- is received for the year 2013-2014 as project funding assistance from various government funding agencies like AICTE, DRDO, Ministry of Defense, ISRO, CSIR, DST, ISTE etc. Memorandum of Understanding is signed with various industries like SEIMENS, L& T, Texas Instruments, Bangalore FICE - Intel India Pvt. Ltd., Bangalore, coreEL Technologies, PRICOL Ltd, PRICOL Technologies, Zylog India Pvt. Ltd, KKM Software Pvt. Ltd. National Instruments, Bangalore, CISCO Networks Academy, Kyungpook National University, South Korea, EMC Corporation etc to set up labs and offer training programmes. A Centre of Excellence in Virtual Instrumentation is established in collaboration with National Instruments Bangalore at the cost of Rs.1.36 crores. A high end research facility in Nanoscience and Technology for characterization and synthesis facilities with imported equipments has been set up at a total cost of Rs.3.6 crores. SREC strives to contribute to Nation building technocrats with sound value system. Our approach to “Leave a Tail than follow a path” is reflected in the rich feedback received from industries enjoying our alumni’s contribution.
